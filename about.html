About Us

Random Snapshots are those which do not need any professionalism to click. They are never taken into consideration and
are always removed from the devices. But if we noticed them carefully, they reflects the beauty of an object very efficiently.
Nature objects are the best example of this. So, to reflect the importance of random snapshots, we introduce Irregular Snaps.
At Irregular Snaps , we provide the best possible content regarding various nature objects(clouds,flowers,etc) through
random snapshots. The snapshots are purely taken by smartphones, not from any other gadget.
